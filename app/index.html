A DEMANDED PRODUCT

Our product on the market is an international network of children's football clubs, in which more than 2,000 children are already engaged. We proved our relevance and uniqueness, therefore we plan to confidently take the leading positions in the niche of children's football in Ukraine, Russia, Kazakhstan, Belarus, Canada and the USA. What is our uniqueness?

We are the only ones in the world who were able to develop a unique hybrid system of modern successful business, which united three completely different systems: sports, private business and block-technology on its platform. We’ve developed and are bringing to the market two unique crypto-coins, which by virtue of their algorithm, have the potential for constant growth. The first is the FBT (Footbik Token) token, which is a token share and entitles its owners to receive dividends every three months from the operation of our network of clubs, and the second is the FBC (Footbik Coin) coin, which is internal unit of account within the system. Buying anything inside the system is possible only with the help of this FBC coin. At the same time, all transactions will be displayed in the blockchain system, which allows the investor to control the cash flows within the system. We already have 9 clubs that work and bring a stable profit now. Why do we conduct ICO? When we can grow rapidly and expand at our own expense. The answer is obvious: it takes much more time. And since we want to take a leading position in the niche of children's football, then we need a sharp leap. And it can only be achieved by attracting investment. To achieve our goals and the opening of 100 clubs, we collect only half of necessary amount. The second half we cover with our profits.

 FAQ

Why is our offer unique?

We managed to combine on one platform a profitable private business, sport and blockchain technology. Our offer is a reliable and profitable investment for those who are accustomed to investing in promising start-ups. But unlike start-ups, our business model is already tested, it works and brings a stable income. In addition to the growth of the tokens, your profit will consist of dividends that will be accrued on the FBT tokens.

What happens if I want to give tokens back?

As our tokens are an analogue of shares and give the opportunity to receive dividends, we are open to redemption of any amount of tokens from investors after six months at the same price as they were purchased during the ICO. First of all, it saves us on dividend payments, and also it is an increase in our share of profits.

Why does it take so long to Why does it take so long to gain profit?

In venture investments the investment return period can reach 10-20 years. We have the full payback of your investment of 2.5 years. This is much faster than investing in real estate and the stock market. In addition to that you have tokens, which are constantly growing in price and which you can sell at any time at the exchange.

Where are you going to open football clubs?

At the first stages we are going to open clubs in Ukraine. Then the clubs will be opened in Kazakhstan, Belarus, Estonia. The next stage in the development of our network will be the opening of clubs in the US and Canada. The ultimate goal is the opening of 100 clubs.

How are dividends accrued?

Dividends are accrued in the local currency FootbikCoin (FBC), which at the request of the investor can instantly be exchanged in a private office on ETH or BTC, or at fiat currency.

Why are you sure that there will be demand for your services?

This is a growing market. Demand in the segment of quality preschool education is growing and many parents are thinking about the future of their children, about how to lay the groundwork for the future profession, how to raise a child as a leader and a confident person. And parents are ready to pay for that, while they understand that investment in their own children is the best possible investment. There are no quality services in the football market for preschoolers, so our goal is to take a leading position in this niche.
