THE MARKETABLE PRODUCT

It is a network of Ukrainian football clubs, which are attended by more than 2000 children. We have already proved our demand, and we plan to occupy confidently a big niche of children's football clubs in Ukraine and CIS countries. 

Now we have 9 clubs that work and make a profit. Why do we conduct an ICO? We can grow rapidly and expand at our own expense,but it will undoubtedly take more time. And we can attract investment and make a sharp leap. For the development of the network, we collect only half of the required amount - the second half we invest from our revenues, so we offer a fair return of 50% of future profits to investors, in accordance with their contribution to the development of the company.

 FAQ

Why is our offer unique?

Our offer is an understandable investment for those who are accustomed to investing in stocks and start-ups. But unlike start-ups, our business model is already tasted and works. You do not need to wait for the price of the token to increase, you will get your profit for possessing of tokens, as a return on investment. Why does it take such a long period to get profit? In venture investments the investment return period can reach 10-20 years. Our full return on investment is equal to the period of 5 years. It is faster than investing in start-ups and the stock market.

How does the distribution of dividends work ?

Dividends are calculated in the form of FootbikCoin tokens, which can be freely converted into ETH or BTC in your personal account.

What happens if I decide to return your tokens?

Since our tokens are an analogue of shares and give the opportunity to receive dividends, we are open to redeeming any volumes of tokens from investors after a year at the same price as they were purchased from the ICO. It would even allow us to save on dividends with further development of the company.

Where are you going to open the clubs?

At first we are going to open clubs in Ukraine. Then the clubs will be opened in the CIS countries like Russia, Kazakhstan, Belarus. The next step will be opening the clubs in the US and Canada.

Why are you sure that there will be demand for your services?

It is a growing market. As the demand for quality preschool and school education is growing, many parents are thinking about and chose different clubs and coteries which could help their children to get some helpful skills for their future life and jobs. In the football market for preschoolers there are no qualitative networks of sport clubs so we can occupy this niche.
